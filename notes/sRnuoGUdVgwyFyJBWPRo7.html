Tea Time

We are officially launching #teatime for all Dendron contributors.

From Teatime
Go to text â†’

Teatime is a channel (both voice and text) for Dendron contributors.

When you join the #teatime channel, it means that you are down for having a conversation with a fellow dendrologist.

The teatime channel is available to anyone that has a role in the Dendron community.

You're free to discuss anything at all in the #teatime channel. To help, we encourage using the pinned read in the Dendron Reading Series as a starting point.
